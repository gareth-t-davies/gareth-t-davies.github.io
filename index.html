Overview

I'm a postdoc at the University of Wuppertal, working in Tibor Jager's IT Security and Cryptography group, as part of the REWOCRYPT project. My research focus is cryptographic protocols, with a recent lean towards updatable encryption and topics related to secure storage. I will serve on the program committee for CT-RSA 2022.
