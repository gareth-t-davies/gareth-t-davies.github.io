Overview

Since February 2023 I have been working for NXP Semiconductors in Leuven as part of the cryptography team led by Joppe Bos. Before that I was a postdoc at the University of Wuppertal, working in Tibor Jager's IT Security and Cryptography group, as part of the REWOCRYPT project.

You can find out about the NXP PQC team's work on post-quantum crypto migration in our recent .

I recently on committees for CT-RSA 2023, SSR 2023 and CCS 2023, and I will serve on the committee for PKC 2025.
