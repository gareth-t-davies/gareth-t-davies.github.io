Overview

I'm a postdoc at the University of Paderborn, working in Tibor Jager's IT-Security group, as part of the REWOCRYPT project. My research focus is cryptographic protocols, with a recent lean towards secure messaging, updatable encryption and oblivious RAM. I will serve on the program committee for SAC 2019 later this year.
